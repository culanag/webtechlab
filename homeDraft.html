Animal Welfare

What is Animal Welfare?

Animal Welfare is concerned with the condition of animals.

What do we aim for?

Animal Welfare envisions the proper treatment of animals.

What does the proper treatment of animals signify?

Humans are animals but it seems as though we view ourselves as Gods compared to them. It is good if we treat them equally as the living beings they are.
