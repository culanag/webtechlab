Animal Welfare

Animal Welfare

Animal welfare pertains to the well-being of an animal. It comprises of the state of the animal physically and mentally In Fort Del Pilar they take this seriously, FDP is a barangay that is surrounded by several species of animals such as dogs, cat, birds, cows, chicken, and other more kinds of animals

Stop the abuse !

This is our advocacy to stop the abuse!

Whether it is a dog or a cat or another animal they have feelings. They have rights, they have emotions. They are family, you won't hurt your family right ? There are cases of abused animals around the world. Killing, hurting, hitting, and other inhumane acts This is our purpose to stop the abuse! We want every species of animals to live peacefuly and be comfortable. Away from suffering, pain, punishments, cruelty, fear and distress.

Together

Barangay Fort Del Pilar is with us in our advocacy to Stop the abuse! Together we can finally put a stop ! animal abuse will be gone. With plan and programs regarding animal welfare together we can make a change.
Our pets have always been there for us,now it is our turn. Let us be their voice in our world Ensuring that the animal's welfare is in good state is the responsibility of every pet owner of every animal.

Essentials for animals

For an animal's welfare to be considered excellent here are some things to remember. As a pet owner your pet should have a shelter, food, and water. You need to provide these for them to healthy. Next is hygiene of your pet It is your responsibility to bathe your pet if you have to, this will make them more comfortable. Having your pet checked by a vet is also helpful promoting your pets animal welfare.

Fort del Pilar

We chose Barangay Fort Del Pilar to be our starting project because FDP is surrounded by animals and away from the city. We wanted to know if FDP is promoting the laws and policy being governed by our city even if it is located in the outskirts of the city. Upon interviewing several people in the barangay including kagawads, barangay officials, residents and commoners, we came to a conclusion that Barangay Fort Del Pilar is a place for both humans and animals. The animals's welfare in this part of the city is in good state. We want to be part of this project and help promote our advocacy and continue our fight against animal abuse.

Happiness

Last January 29, 2018 barangay Fort Del Pilar had their annual anti-rabies vaccination. Residents and their Pets went to designated locations in Fort Del Pilar to have free vaccinations for their pets.

Upon interview we gathered information that FDP conducts Annual vaccinations for animals. Free of charge and for every pets in the barangay. Just last January 29, alongside with the staff of Baguio Vetineray Office, FDP had its 2018 Anti-Rabbies Vaccination. This event was monitored by the barangay officials and the satff of the vetineray office. Which was held in the Barangay hall's gate, Basketball court and the Barangay garden
