Our Advocacy

We are mainly concerned on the condition of the animals. It is our priority that animals in Barangay Fort Del Pilar are treated the way they deserve.

Our Mission

Our advocacy is intended to propagate issues and ideas so as to solve existing problems while enhancing the state of animal welfare that there already is.

Our Vision

We envision that one day every animal in Barangay Fort Del Pilar is at the best condition possible and there exists a harmony between the said animals and the citizens.
