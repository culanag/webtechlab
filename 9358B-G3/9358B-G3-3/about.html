About Us

We are a group of students who want to do something for the greater good. We cannot change the world immediately but we can change it one place at a time. We started with Barangay Fort Del Pilar since it is a place where two of our group members are already familiar with.

Currently, we are studying BSIT in Saint Louis University and this website is a project which was given to us. Regardless of this being a requirement, we desire to spread the word on different advocacies and make a positive impact in this great country that we live in. We hope that we have influenced you to follow our advocacy.
